What's a Mechatronics Engineer

Mechatronics Engineering is a field of engineering that combines and unifies the key areas of mechanical, electronic and electrical systems. This means I have stuided some of the same material as mechanical and electrical engineers. The main applications of such a degree are in automation, robotics, consumer products, transport, PLCs and microprocessors. A Mechatronics specialist may take on the job titles of Software Engineer, Project Engineer, Automotive Engineer or Instrumentation Engineer. Tasks may inlcude designing, developing, maintaining and manageing: high-technology systems for automation, consumer products, mechanical processors where human labour may be dangerous and feasibility studies.
